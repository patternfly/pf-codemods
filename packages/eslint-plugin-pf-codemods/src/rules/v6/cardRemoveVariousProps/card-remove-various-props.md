### card-remove-various-props [(#10056)](https://github.com/patternfly/patternfly-react/pull/10056)

The following props have been removed from Card:
- isSelectableRaised
- isDisabledRaised
- hasSelectableInput
- selectableInputAriaLabel
- onSelectableInputChange
- isFlat
- isRounded

In:

```jsx
%inputExample%
```
